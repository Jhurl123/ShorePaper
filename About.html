Shore Paper Box

Eastern Shore's best set up box maker!

Check us out on Travels with Charlie! WBOC-TV 16, Delmarvas News Leader, FOX 21

In 1953, Pocomoke Paper Box Company began business in Pocomoke City, MD. The main product of the time was boxes for the Eastern Shore shirt factories.
In 1957 the box company was relocated to Mardela Springs, MD and started doing business at Shore Paper Box Co.
As time progressed the shirt manufacturers on the Eastern Shore became fewer. During this time Shore Paper Box branched out to service the packaging needs of the confectionary, pharmaceutical and department store industries, as well as making custom boxes for each individual customers needs not only on the Eastern Shore, but world-wide.
